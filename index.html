Basic Information About Cars

A car contains dozens of different technologies. Everything from the engine to the tires is its own special universe of design and engineering.A car is a road automobile used to carry passengers (people). Cars usually have four wheels, and an engine or motor to make them move. Cars are also called automobiles, which comes from the Greek prefix "αυτό" (auto) and the French word "mobile". This name means "self-moving", as cars run on their own power.
